Abstract

We propose Mutual-Information Unlearning (MUITR), an information-theoretic regularizer that forces an LLM to forget a target corpus while preserving utility on the retained data. ⋯

Method

We minimize \\[ \mathcal{L} = (1-\gamma)\, \mathrm{KL}\bigl(p_\theta(\cdot\!\mid\!R)\,\|\,p_{\theta_0}(\cdot\!\mid\!R)\bigr) \;+\; \gamma\, I_\theta(X;Z), \\] ⋯
